Frequently Asked Questions

How does the design system extraction work?

We use computer vision and DOM analysis to ingest the visual properties of the URLs you provide. This includes font stacks, color contrast ratios, border radii, and spacing scales.

Can I export the code?

Yes! You can export as a React/Next.js project, raw HTML/CSS, or deploy directly to our edge network.

Is the generated code accessible?

Absolutely. SiteFlow prioritizes WCAG 2.1 AA standards, ensuring proper semantic HTML, aria-labels, and contrast ratios.
