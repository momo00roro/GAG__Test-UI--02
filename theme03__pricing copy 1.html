Frequently Asked Questions

What is included in the free plan?

The free plan includes 3 projects, 1GB storage, community support, and basic analytics to get you started with SiteFlow.

Can I change or cancel my plan anytime?

Yes, you can upgrade, downgrade, or cancel your plan at any time. Changes take effect immediately, and we offer a 30-day money-back guarantee.

Is there a discount for annual billing?

Yes, save 20% when you choose annual billing on any paid plan. This applies to both Pro and Enterprise tiers.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit cards, PayPal, and bank transfers for Enterprise customers. All payments are processed securely.

Do you offer enterprise discounts?

Yes, custom pricing is available for organizations with specific needs. Contact our sales team for a tailored quote and potential discounts.
